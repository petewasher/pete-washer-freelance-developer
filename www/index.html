What can Cyclick do for you?

Cyclick is a small development company specialising in Backend development. We primarily write in NodeJS, with a focus on producing efficient, conscience and easy to maintain applications which solve problems for businesses.

We were founded in 2018 by Pete Washer. Pete is a Senior Software Engineer with many years experience developing across the stack - from Embedded projects with British manufacturing market leaders Instron Ltd. to working with small businesses like Veiled Productions to complete customer facing projects.

Though we work across varied markets, we always strive to maintain quality in our work through:

  • Excellent customer service
  • Succinct, useful and accurate API documentation
  • Self-documenting code
  • Employing the latest technologies where appropriate
  • Thoroughly testing applications
  • Deploying early and often
  • Automation of the boring stuff
